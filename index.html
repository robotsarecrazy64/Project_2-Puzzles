The Lost Dude

Instructions

You have fallen into a deep dungeon.

Press W to move up, S to move down, A to move left, and D to move right.

Click on the harmless critters to gain more energy. Some critters will defend themselves when attacked.

Surviving critters will run away, and dead critters give random energy (MAX 10).

Try to beat the game without killing anything.

Pick the right door in order to advance to the next floor until you have found your way back to the surface.

Each incorrectly chosen door will cost you energy. If you run out of energy, you will faint.
